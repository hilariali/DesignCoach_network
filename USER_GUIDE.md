## Getting Started

### Overview
BusinessMatch Pro is a comprehensive business matching platform that connects entrepreneurs, experts, and team members to build successful startups. The platform features:

- **Expert Discovery**: Find and connect with industry experts
- **Team Formation**: Create and manage startup teams
- **AI Coaching**: Get business guidance from our AI coach
- **Real-time Chat**: Communicate with team members
- **Business Model Canvas**: Generate and refine your business strategy

### System Requirements
- Modern web browser (Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+)
- Internet connection
- Screen resolution: 1024x768 or higher

## Authentication

### Registration
1. Click "Sign up" on the login page
2. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email address
   - Phone number (optional)
   - Password
   - Your expectation (team up vs work for others)
3. Click "Create Account"
4. Alternatively, use SSO with Google, LinkedIn, or GitHub

### Login
1. Enter your email and password
2. Click "Sign In"
3. Or use SSO options for quick access

### Password Recovery
- Click "Forgot password" on the login page
- Enter your email address
- Follow the instructions sent to your email

## User Profile

### Viewing Your Profile
Your profile showcases:
- **Basic Information**: Name, photo, bio
- **Expertise Domains**: Your areas of expertise
- **Target Customers**: Who you serve
- **Resources & Assets**: What you bring to the table
- **Track Records**: Your achievements and milestones
- **Rating**: Multi-aspect rating based on your activity

### Editing Your Profile
1. Navigate to Profile → Edit Profile
2. Update your information:
   - **Profile Picture**: Upload a professional photo
   - **Bio**: Describe yourself and your goals
   - **Expertise**: Select from available domains
   - **Target Customers**: Define your audience
   - **Resources**: List your assets
   - **Track Records**: Add achievements
3. Click "Save Changes"

### Profile Completeness
- Completeness is calculated automatically
- Higher completeness improves visibility
- Aim for 80%+ for best results

### Expert Badge
- **Expert** status is granted by the platform
- Cannot be self-obtained
- Indicates verified expertise and experience

## Finding Experts

### Search Functionality
1. Go to "Find Experts" from the navigation
2. Use the search bar to find by name
3. Apply filters:
   - **Expertise Domain**: Filter by skills
   - **Target Customers**: Filter by market focus

### Understanding Search Results
Each profile card shows:
- Name and expert badge
- Brief bio
- Expertise tags
- Rating score

### Connecting with Experts

2. Click "Chat Request" to initiate contact

- Access saved profiles from your profile page

## Team Management

### Creating a Team
1. Go to "My Teams" page
2. Click "Create Team"
3. Enter:
   - Team name
   - Description
4. Click "Create"

### Team Profile
Each team has:
- **Members**: List of all team members with roles
- **Combined Expertise**: Aggregated skills from all members
- **Combined Resources**: Pooled assets and connections
- **Achievements**: Team accomplishments
- **Milestones**: Project timeline and goals
- **Business Model Canvas**: Strategic framework

### Inviting Members
1. Open team profile
2. Share team ID or invite link
3. Members can request to join

### Managing Milestones
1. Go to Team → Milestones tab
2. View completed and upcoming milestones
3. Track progress with visual indicators

## Rating System

### How Ratings Work
Your rating is calculated automatically based on:

1. **Membership Time** (0-5)
   - Longer membership = higher score
   - Based on months since joining

2. **Teams Joined** (0-5)
   - More teams = higher score
   - Shows collaboration experience

3. **Successful Projects** (0-5)
   - Completed projects boost score
   - Demonstrates execution ability

4. **Offline Trainings** (0-5)
   - Attended workshops/events
   - Shows commitment to learning

### Overall Rating
- Average of all four aspects
- Displayed on your profile
- Helps others evaluate your credibility

### Improving Your Rating
- Stay active on the platform
- Join and contribute to teams
- Complete projects and mark milestones
- Attend training sessions
